Modern encryption protocols are practically unbreakable, this ensures that the content of the communication is protected, but metadata and traffic patterns can still be exploited to infer information. 
The research examines how protocols such as TLS, IPsec, HTTPS secure network communications. 
We then investigated different techniques used in network analysis to extract intelligence without breaking encryption such as Leakage Vectors, Inversion attacks, Membership inference attacks. 
Many of these exploits utilize machine learning to learn the patterns of packets and user data, the process of learning patterns can result in vectorization of personal data, its necessary to ensure that no leaks are the result of improper training of machine learning models.  
Military or government communications require additional network security, the report answers why encryption alone is not enough in this context.
